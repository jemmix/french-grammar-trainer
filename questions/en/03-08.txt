ID: 03-08-001
TYPE: MCQ
PROMPT: Which sentence correctly shows a background action and a main event?
RIGHT ANSWER: The sun was shining when she arrived at the beach.
EXPLANATION: Past continuous "was shining" sets the background scene; past simple "arrived" is the main event.
WRONG ANSWER: The sun shone when she was arriving at the beach.
EXPLANATION: This reverses the pattern. The background should be continuous, the main event simple.
WRONG ANSWER: The sun was shining when she was arriving at the beach.
EXPLANATION: Both in past continuous suggests simultaneous ongoing actions, not background vs. main event.
WRONG ANSWER: The sun shone when she arrived at the beach.
EXPLANATION: Both in past simple loses the background-main event distinction.
END QUESTION
