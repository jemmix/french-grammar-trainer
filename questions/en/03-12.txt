ID: 03-12-001
TYPE: MCQ
PROMPT: Which sentence shows annoyance with "always" in the past?
RIGHT ANSWER: He was always leaving his dirty dishes in the sink.
EXPLANATION: Past continuous with "always" expresses annoyance about a repeated past habit.
WRONG ANSWER: He always left his dirty dishes in the sink.
EXPLANATION: Past simple with "always" states a fact without the annoyance connotation.
WRONG ANSWER: He always leaves his dirty dishes in the sink.
EXPLANATION: Present simple describes a current habit, not a past one.
WRONG ANSWER: He has always left his dirty dishes in the sink.
EXPLANATION: Present perfect describes a continuing situation, not past annoyance.
END QUESTION
