PROMPT: Complete the sentence: "___ you sleeping when I called?"
RIGHT ANSWER: Were
EXPLANATION: With "you," past continuous questions use "Were" + subject + verb-ing. "Were you sleeping" is correct.
WRONG ANSWER: Was
EXPLANATION: "Was" is used with I/he/she/it. With "you," always use "Were."
WRONG ANSWER: Did
EXPLANATION: "Did" is used with the base form (Did you sleep?). Past continuous questions use "Were."
WRONG ANSWER: Are
EXPLANATION: "Are" is present tense. Past continuous questions use "Were."
